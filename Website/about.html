About the Website Eye Robot

The Website Eye Robot is a cutting-edge project aimed at identifying and reporting design issues on responsive websites. Our solution involves building a system that scans a website at different resolutions, identifies design issues, and presents them to developers in a report. Our system includes complicated algorithms, image processing techniques and machine learning models to help identify different types of design issues automatically.

Why the Website Eye Robot is Important

The Website Eye Robot has the potential to revolutionize the way we approach responsive web design. By automating the process of identifying design issues, our system can help developers save time and improve the quality of their work. This, in turn, can lead to better user experiences and increased customer satisfaction. Additionally, our system can help businesses reduce the risk of costly design errors and improve their website's overall performance.

Our Mission

At the Website Eye Robot project, our mission is to make responsive web design more accessible and effective. We believe that by providing developers with powerful tools to identify design issues, we can help them create better websites that meet the needs of their users. To achieve our mission, we are constantly researching and developing new technologies, working closely with our partners in the industry, and collaborating with academic institutions to stay on the cutting edge of web design innovation.
